One-on-one Dream Interpretation

Dreams offer direct access to the unconscious mind. Working with dreams is a powerful way to solve creative problems, overcome blocks, reduce inhibiting self-judgments. Dreams often appear enigmatic, confusing or meaningless, but they can be interpreted using the simple technique of free association. In our one-on-one sessions you will freely associate to the elements of your dream, saying whatever comes to mind without censorship. I listen attentively and without judgement, helping you unravel the threads of images and ideas your dreams contain. Interpreting a dream through free association slowly reveals the deeper patterns and structures of your unconscious thought. Once learned, this powerful practice can be carried on individually, as a part of your creative practice, or in one-on-one sessions as desired.
